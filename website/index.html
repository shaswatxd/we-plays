Fully Loaded with Premium Features

Experience a music player engineered for audiophiles, with advanced offline functionality.

🚀

In-App YouTube Downloader

Search YouTube and YT Music without leaving the player. Pick any result, choose your format and quality, and it lands in your library within seconds — powered by an embedded yt-dlp engine with up to three parallel downloads at once.

🔗

Paste a Link, Get the Music

Drop any YouTube song or full playlist link into Settings and We Plays fetches every track for you. See exactly how many songs will be downloaded, watch live per-song progress, and let the built-in queue handle the rest — even for hundred-track playlists.

🎚️

Your Format, Your Quality

Every download is converted by FFmpeg to the format you choose — MP3, lossless FLAC, AAC, or OGG — at bitrates from 128 up to 320 kbps, with album artwork and full ID3 metadata tags embedded automatically.

🎨

Dynamic Theme Engine

The interface repaints itself around your music. Gradient colors are extracted from the album art of the current song, so every track gives the whole app a matching mood.

🎤

Synchronized Lyrics

Fetch real-time lyrics for the song you're playing and follow along line by line, perfectly in sync with the audio.

📚

Free LibriVox Audiobooks

Browse thousands of free public-domain audiobooks in a dedicated player — variable speed (0.5x–3x), sleep timer, chapter navigation, resume-where-you-left-off, timestamp bookmarks, and offline chapter downloads.

📊

Listening Insights

A personal analytics dashboard built from your real listening history: top songs, favorite artists, hour-by-hour listening patterns, and total playtime.

🗂️

Playlists & Smart Library

Create playlists, drag to reorder the queue, mark favorites, and rate songs. Import your existing music by dropping files or whole folders onto the window — tags, duration, and album info are read automatically.

🧹

One-Click Library Cleaner

A single scan finds duplicate songs, duplicate playlists, and entries whose files have gone missing from disk — then lets you review and remove them safely with checkboxes, not guesswork.

⌨️

Shortcuts & Tray Control

Control everything from the keyboard — play/pause, next, shuffle, repeat, volume, search — and keep the music going from the system tray with a live play/pause indicator when the window is closed.

🔄

Always Up to Date

One-click updates for the app itself, plus built-in updaters for yt-dlp and FFmpeg — so downloads keep working even when YouTube changes things behind the scenes.

📴

Truly Offline

Everything you download is a real audio file on your own disk, organized in a local SQLite library. No streaming, no subscription, no internet needed to play your music.

How It Works

From a link to a fully tagged offline library in four steps — no accounts, no configuration.

1️⃣

Install & Open

Run the Windows installer. yt-dlp, FFmpeg, and the local database are bundled inside, so the app is fully working the moment it opens.

2️⃣

Search or Paste a Link

Search any song inside the app, or paste a YouTube song or playlist link in Settings. We Plays lists every track it found and tells you exactly how many will be downloaded.

3️⃣

Choose Quality & Folder

Pick MP3, FLAC, AAC, or OGG, set the bitrate up to 320 kbps, and choose where the files are saved. yt-dlp grabs the audio and FFmpeg converts it, embedding artwork and metadata tags.

4️⃣

Play — Forever, Offline

Finished downloads appear in your library instantly, with live progress while they run. From there it's all yours: playlists, lyrics, stats, and playback that never needs the internet again.
